# D&C 102:8

> Voted: that whenever any vacancy shall occur by the death, removal from office for transgression, or removal from the bounds of this church government, of any one of the above-named councilors, it shall be filled by the nomination of the <u>president</u>[^a] or presidents, and sanctioned by the voice of a general council of high priests, convened for that purpose, to act in the name of the church.
