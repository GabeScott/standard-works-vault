# D&C 128:22

> Brethren, shall we not go on in so great a cause? Go forward and not backward. <u>Courage</u>[^a], brethren; and on, on to the victory! Let your hearts rejoice, and be exceedingly glad. Let the earth break forth into <u>singing</u>[^b] . Let the <u>dead</u>[^c] speak forth anthems of eternal praise to the <u>King</u>[^d] Immanuel, who hath ordained, before the world was, that which would enable us to <u>redeem</u>[^e] them out of their <u>prison</u>[^f]; for the prisoners shall go free.
