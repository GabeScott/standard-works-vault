# D&C 20:1

> The <u>rise</u>[^a] of the <u>Church</u>[^b] of Christ in these last days, being one thousand eight hundred and thirty years since the <u>coming</u>[^c] of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ in the flesh, it being regularly <u>organized</u>[^d] and established agreeable to the <u>laws</u>[^e] of our country, by the will and commandments of God, in the fourth month, and on the sixth day of the month which is called April—

[D&C 20:1](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/20.1?lang=eng#p1)
These first sixteen verses serve to record the establishment of the Church. They recount:
- The calling of Joseph Smith [[20250101apq - D&C 20.2]] and Oliver Cowdery [[20250101apr - D&C 20.3]]
- The first vision [[20250101apt - D&C 20.5]]
- Joseph's meeting with the angel [[20250101apu - D&C 20.6]], [[20250101apv - D&C 20.7]]
- The coming forth of the Book of Mormon [[20250101apw - D&C 20.8]], [[20250101apx - D&C 20.9]]
- The witnesses [[20250101apy - D&C 20.10]], [[20250101apz - D&C 20.11]]
