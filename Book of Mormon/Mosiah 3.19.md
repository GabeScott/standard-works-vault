# Mosiah 3:19

> For the <u>natural</u>[^a] <u>man</u>[^b] is an <u>enemy</u>[^c] to God, and has been from the <u>fall</u>[^d] of Adam, and will be, forever and ever, unless he <u>yields</u>[^e] to the enticings of the <u>Holy Spirit</u>[^f], and <u>putteth</u>[^g] off the <u>natural</u>[^h] man and becometh a <u>saint</u>[^i] through the atonement of Christ the Lord, and becometh as a <u>child</u>[^j], <u>submissive</u>[^k], meek, humble, patient, full of love, willing to submit to all things which the Lord seeth fit to inflict upon him, even as a child doth submit to his father.
