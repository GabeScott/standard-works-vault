# 1 Nephi 19:10

> And the <u>God</u>[^a] of our fathers, who were <u>led</u>[^b] out of Egypt, out of bondage, and also were preserved in the wilderness by him, yea, the <u>God</u>[^c] of Abraham, and of Isaac, and the God of Jacob, <u>yieldeth</u>[^d] himself, according to the words of the angel, as a man, into the hands of <u>wicked</u>[^e] men, to be <u>lifted up</u>[^f], according to the words of <u>Zenock</u>[^g], and to be <u>crucified</u>[^h], according to the words of Neum, and to be buried in a <u>sepulchre</u>[^i], according to the words of <u>Zenos</u>[^j], which he spake concerning the three days of <u>darkness</u>[^k], which should be a sign given of his death unto those who should inhabit the isles of the sea, more especially given unto those who are of the <u>house of Israel</u>[^l] .
