# 1 Timothy 3:15

> <u>But</u>[^a] if I tarry long, that thou mayest know how thou oughtest to behave thyself in the house of God, which is the <u>church</u>[^b] of the living God, the pillar and <u>ground</u>[^c] of the <u>truth</u>[^d] .
