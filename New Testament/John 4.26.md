# John 4:26

> Jesus saith unto her, <u>I</u>[^a] that speak unto thee am he.

[John 4:26](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/4.26?lang=eng#p26)


[^a]: The term I Am used here in the Greek is identical with the Septuagint usage in Ex. 3:14 which identifies Jehovah. John 8:58. TG [Jesus Christ, Jehovah](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/tg/jesus-christ-jehovah?lang=eng).
