# Luke 15:8

> ¶ Either what woman having ten pieces of silver, if she lose one <u>piece</u>[^a], doth not light a candle, and sweep the house, and seek diligently till she find it?

[Luke 15:8](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/luke/15.8?lang=eng#p8)


[^a]: GR drachma (a silver coin equal to the Roman denarius—a workman’s daily wage).
