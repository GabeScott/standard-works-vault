# Isaiah 18:2

> That sendeth ambassadors by the sea, even in vessels of <u>bulrushes</u>[^a] upon the waters, saying, Go, ye swift messengers, to a nation scattered and peeled, to a people terrible from their beginning hitherto; a nation meted out and trodden down, whose land the rivers have <u>spoiled</u>[^b] !

[^a]: HEB reed or papyrus.  
[^b]: HEB cut up or divided.
