# Genesis 6:16

> A <u>window</u>[^a] shalt thou make to the ark, and in a cubit shalt thou finish it above; and the door of the ark shalt thou set in the side thereof; with lower, second, and third stories shalt thou make it.

[Genesis 6:16](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/6.16?lang=eng#p16)


[^a]: HEB tsohar; some rabbis believed it was a precious stone that shone in the ark. Ether 2:23 (23-24).
