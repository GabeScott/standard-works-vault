# Leviticus 25:5

> That which groweth of its own accord of thy harvest thou shalt not reap, neither gather the grapes <u>of thy vine undressed</u>[^a] : for it is a year of rest unto the land.

[Leviticus 25:5](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/lev/25.5?lang=eng#p5)


[^a]: HEB of thy separation; i.e., of the time “set apart” as a sabbatical year.
