# 1 Samuel 24:4

> And the men of David said unto him, Behold the day of which the Lord said unto thee, Behold, I will deliver thine enemy into thine hand, that thou mayest do to him as it shall seem good unto thee. Then David arose, and cut off the <u>skirt</u>[^a] of Saul’s robe <u>privily</u>[^b] .

[1 Samuel 24:4](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/1-sam/24.4?lang=eng#p4)


[^a]: OR hem, corner, border which signifies the portion that symbolized his authority (also [[1 Samuel 24.5|vv. 5, 11]][[1 Samuel 24.11|]]).  
[^b]: OR secretly.
