# Isaiah 2:1

> The word that <u>Isaiah</u>[^a] the son of Amoz <u>saw</u>[^b] concerning Judah and Jerusalem.

[Isaiah 2:1](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/isa/2.1?lang=eng#p1)


[^a]: Isaiah chapters 2-14 are quoted from the brass plates by Nephi in 2 Ne. 12-24; there are some differences in wording which should be noted.
[^b]: HEB khazah, meaning “envisioned.” It means Isaiah received his message through a vision from the Lord.
