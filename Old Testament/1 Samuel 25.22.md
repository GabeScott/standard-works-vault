# 1 Samuel 25:22

> So and more also do God unto the enemies of David, if I leave of all that pertain to him by the morning light <u>any</u>[^a] that pisseth against the wall.

[1 Samuel 25:22](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/1-sam/25.22?lang=eng#p22)


[^a]: What follows is a derisive term for “any male” (also v. 34).
