# Genesis 30:24

> And she called his name <u>Joseph</u>[^a]; and said, The Lord shall add to me another son.

[Genesis 30:24](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/30.24?lang=eng#p24)


[^a]: “Joseph” relates both to the Hebrew root yasaph, “to add,” and to asaph, meaning both “to take away” and “to gather.” The context plays upon all of these meanings. TG [Israel, Joseph, People of](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/tg/israel-joseph-people-of?lang=eng).
