# Isaiah 21:10

> <u>O</u>[^a] my threshing, and the corn of my floor: that which I have heard of the Lord of hosts, the God of Israel, have I declared unto you.

[Isaiah 21:10](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/isa/21.10?lang=eng#p10)


[^a]: HEB O my threshed one and son of my threshing floor. (The prophet thus addressed the Israelites who will survive Babylon’s downfall.)
