# Nehemiah 3:1

> Then Eliashib the <u>high priest</u>[^a] rose up with his brethren the priests, and they builded the <u>sheep gate</u>[^b]; they sanctified it, and set up the doors of it; even unto the tower of Meah they sanctified it, unto the tower of Hananeel.

[^a]: TG [Priesthood, Aaronic.](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/tg/priesthood-aaronic?lang=eng)
[^b]: A gate probably in the northeast wall of Jerusalem near the temple, for access of sacrificial animals.
