# Jeremiah 26:3

> If so be they will hearken, and turn every man from his evil way, that I may <u>repent me</u>[^a] of the evil, which I purpose to do unto them because of the evil of their doings.

[Jeremiah 26:3](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/jer/26.3?lang=eng#p3)


[^a]: HEB relent; i.e., change the decreed punishment because of their changed behavior.
