# Jeremiah 4:30

> And when thou art spoiled, what wilt thou do? Though thou clothest thyself with crimson, though thou deckest thee with <u>ornaments</u>[^a] of gold, though thou <u>rentest thy face</u>[^b] with painting, in vain shalt thou make thyself fair; thy <u>lovers</u>[^c] will despise thee, they will seek thy life.
