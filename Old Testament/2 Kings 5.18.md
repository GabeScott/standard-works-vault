# 2 Kings 5:18

> In this thing the Lord pardon thy servant, that when my master goeth into the house of <u>Rimmon</u>[^a] to worship there, and he leaneth on my hand, and I bow myself in the house of Rimmon: when I bow down myself in the house of Rimmon, the Lord pardon thy servant in this thing.

[2 Kings 5:18](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/2-kgs/5.18?lang=eng#p18)


[^a]: IE a Syrian god of wind, rain, and storm.
