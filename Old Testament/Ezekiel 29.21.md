# Ezekiel 29:21

> ¶ In that day will I cause the horn of the house of Israel to <u>bud forth</u>[^a], and I will give thee the <u>opening of the mouth</u>[^b] in the midst of them; and they shall know that I am the Lord .

[Ezekiel 29:21](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/ezek/29.21?lang=eng#p21)


[^a]: Hebrew metaphor meaning power, capacity.
[^b]: Hebrew idiom meaning “authority to speak.”
