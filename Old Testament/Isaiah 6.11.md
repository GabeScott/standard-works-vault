# Isaiah 6:11

> Then said I, Lord, <u>how</u>[^a] long? And he answered, Until the cities be wasted without inhabitant, and the houses without man, and the land be utterly desolate,

[Isaiah 6:11](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/isa/6.11?lang=eng#p11)


[^a]: The prophet wonders how long men will be so, and the Lord answers: until mortal man is no more.
