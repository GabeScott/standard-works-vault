# Moses 2:10

> And I, God, called the dry land <u>Earth</u>[^a]; and the gathering together of the waters, called I the Sea; and I, God, saw that all things which I had made were good.

[Moses 2:10](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/moses/2.10?lang=eng#p10)


[^a]: IE The whole sphere or any of its parts above the seas is called “Earth.”
