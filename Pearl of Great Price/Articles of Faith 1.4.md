# Articles of Faith 1:4

> We believe that the first principles and <u>ordinances</u>[^a] of the Gospel are: first, <u>Faith</u>[^b] in the Lord Jesus Christ; second, <u>Repentance</u>[^c]; third, <u>Baptism</u>[^d] by <u>immersion</u>[^e] for the <u>remission</u>[^f] of sins; fourth, Laying on of <u>hands</u>[^g] for the <u>gift</u>[^h] of the Holy Ghost.
