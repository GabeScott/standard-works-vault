# Joseph Smith History 1:68

> We still continued the work of translation, when, in the ensuing month (May, 1829), we on a certain day went into the woods to pray and inquire of the Lord respecting <u>baptism</u>[^a] for the <u>remission</u>[^b] of sins, that we found mentioned in the translation of the plates. While we were thus employed, praying and calling upon the Lord, a messenger from heaven descended in a <u>cloud</u>[^c] of light, and having laid his <u>hands</u>[^d] upon us, he <u>ordained</u>[^e] us, saying:
